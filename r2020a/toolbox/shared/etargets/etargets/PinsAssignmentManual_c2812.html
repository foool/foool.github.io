GPIO A MUX Control

GPAMUX

     BIT           Peripheral Name (bit = 1)             GPIO Name (bit = 0)

EV-A Peripheral                    

     0                    (PWM1)                                GPIOA0

     1                    (PWM2)                                GPIOA1

     2                    (PWM3)                                GPIOA2

     3                    (PWM4)                                GPIOA3

     4                    (PWM5)                                GPIOA4

     5                    (PWM6)                                GPIOA5

    

     8                    (QEP1/CAP1)                        GPIOA8          

     9                    (QEP2/CAP2)                        GPIOA9

   10                    (CAP3)                                  GPIOA10

 

GPIO B MUX Control

GPBMUX

     BIT           Peripheral Name (bit = 1)             GPIO Name (bit = 0)

EV-B Peripheral                    

     0                    (PWM7)                                GPIOB0

     1                    (PWM8)                                GPIOB1

     2                    (PWM9)                                GPIOB2

     3                    (PWM10)                              GPIOB3

     4                    (PWM11)                              GPIOB4

     5                    (PWM12)                              GPIOB5

    

     8                    (QEP3/CAP4)                        GPIOB8          

     9                    (QEP4/CAP5)                        GPIOB9

   10                    (CAP6)                                  GPIOB10
